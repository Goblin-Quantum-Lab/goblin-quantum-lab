# Support Subjects

Support Subjects are standalone helper courses for learners who want extra preparation or review alongside Goblin Quantum Lab pathway courses.

They are not part of the main learning pathways. They are not formal prerequisites by default. Learners may take them before, during, or after pathway courses.

Pathway courses may recommend a Support Subject as helpful preparation when a topic would make the main course easier to follow.

## How To Use These Courses

Use Support Subjects when a pathway course mentions a skill you want to strengthen. You can also use them independently if you are building entry-level readiness for open-source technical learning.
