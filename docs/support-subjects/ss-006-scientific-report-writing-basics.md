# SS-006: Scientific Report Writing Basics

## Overview

This standalone helper course introduces the structure and habits of clear scientific and technical reports.

## Helpful For

- Writing objectives, methods, results, and conclusions
- Describing limitations honestly
- Separating observation from interpretation
- Preparing beginner lab reports

## Relationship To Pathway Courses

This course is not a formal prerequisite by default. It may be recommended alongside lab or report-writing tasks.
