# SS-008: Research Evidence and Reproducibility Basics

## Overview

This standalone helper course introduces evidence, reproducibility, and cautious claims in technical learning.

## Helpful For

- Recording methods clearly
- Understanding limitations
- Distinguishing evidence from speculation
- Building entry-level readiness for open-source technical work

## Relationship To Pathway Courses

This course is not a formal prerequisite by default. It may be recommended for learners preparing for report tasks or project-style work.
