# Trademark and Brand Use Policy

:::info
Goblin Quantum Lab is independent. References to third-party tools, platforms, operating systems, or services are for identification and compatibility purposes only.
:::

## Purpose

Goblin Quantum Lab uses its own name, logo, badges, course marks, visual identity, and educational materials as protected project assets. This policy explains how Goblin Quantum Lab identifies third-party tools and platforms without implying endorsement, sponsorship, affiliation, or partnership.

## Goblin Quantum Lab Protected Marks

Goblin Quantum Lab, GQL, course names, badge names, badge designs, logos, icons, course artwork, slide templates, visual identity, and educational branding are protected project assets unless explicitly stated otherwise.

## No Unauthorised Use

Do not copy, reuse, modify, redistribute, publish, sell, or create derivative products from Goblin Quantum Lab logos, badges, course marks, templates, visual identity, or branding without written permission.

## Third-Party Names

Third-party names such as Windows, macOS, Ubuntu, Linux, WSL, Python, Qiskit, Jupyter, GitHub, IBM, AWS, Azure, and similar names may appear in Goblin Quantum Lab materials only to identify relevant tools, operating systems, setup paths, or compatibility information.

## No Endorsement

Use of third-party names does not imply endorsement, sponsorship, approval, partnership, or affiliation with the trademark owners.

## No Third-Party Logo Use By Default

Goblin Quantum Lab does not use third-party company or operating-system logos in course slides, learner pages, setup paths, badges, or marketing materials unless written permission or a clearly applicable brand-use licence has been recorded.

## Operating System Path Labels

Operating-system paths are identified using plain text labels, such as:

- Operating System Path: Windows 10/11
- Operating System Path: macOS
- Operating System Path: Ubuntu/Linux
- Operating System Path: WSL on Windows
- Operating System Path: Chromebook / Locked-Down Device

## Learner And Contributor Warning

Learners and contributors must not upload, submit, or contribute third-party logos, screenshots, copyrighted diagrams, copied course materials, copied textbook content, or protected brand assets unless they have permission and clearly identify the source and licence.

## Course Content Protection

Goblin Quantum Lab educational content, course structures, slide decks, lab briefs, assessments, badges, logos, and branding are protected unless explicitly licensed otherwise.

## Reporting Concerns

If you believe Goblin Quantum Lab material misuses a third-party mark or protected asset, open a public issue using the appropriate contact/reporting route. Do not include private credentials, tokens, personal identification documents, or confidential information in the report.
