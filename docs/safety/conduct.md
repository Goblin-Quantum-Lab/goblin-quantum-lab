# Code of Conduct for Labs

This document outlines the expected conduct within the Goblin Quantum Lab environment, specifically for practical lab sessions.

## Principles

- **Respect**: Treat all participants, instructors, and resources with respect.
- **Collaboration**: Encourage and support collaborative learning, but ensure individual understanding.
- **Integrity**: Maintain academic honesty in all lab work and reporting.
- **Safety**: Adhere to all safety guidelines and procedures.
- **Responsibility**: Take ownership of your actions and their impact on others and the learning environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Engage in constructive discussions and provide helpful feedback.
- Be inclusive and welcoming to all participants.
- Follow all instructions and guidelines provided for each lab.
- Report any issues or concerns promptly to the lab instructors or maintainers.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or any form of disrespectful conduct.
- Cheating, plagiarism, or unauthorized sharing of solutions.
- Disrupting the learning environment or interfering with others' work.
- Any actions that compromise the integrity or safety of the lab environment.

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience any violations of this Code of Conduct, please report them to the project maintainers or lab instructors. All reports will be handled with discretion and appropriate action will be taken.
