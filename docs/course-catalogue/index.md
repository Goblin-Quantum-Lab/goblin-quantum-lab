# Course Catalogue

Goblin Quantum Lab provides open Support Subjects and enrollment-bound quantum courses. Support materials are public. Full course materials, labs, assignments, and assessed work are released only through the enrolled learner process.

Protected course materials and assessment workflows are maintained outside the public repository.

## Public Boundary

Public catalogue pages may describe a course, its status, expected preparation, and enrollment route. They must not publish full course packs, assignments, tests, protected assessment materials, protected grading materials, reviewer notes, badge decision records, private process details, private storage details.
