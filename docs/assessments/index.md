# Assessment Access

Goblin Quantum Lab assessment information is public only at the overview level.

## Public Assessment Information

Public pages may explain assessment purpose, access boundaries, badge review timelines, and enrollment requirements.

## Enrolled-Only Assessment Materials

Full assessment prompts, tests, assignments, submission instructions, learner-specific review instructions, and assessed work are released only through the enrolled learner process.

## Never Public

Protected assessment and grading materials, reviewer notes, badge decision records, and private process details are never published in the public repository.

## Badge Boundary

GQL badges are local learning milestones only. They are not accredited credentials, university credits, professional licences, or formal certifications. Badge review is manual and may take up to 30 business days.

See [Public vs Enrolled Access](/docs/course-access/public-vs-enrolled).
