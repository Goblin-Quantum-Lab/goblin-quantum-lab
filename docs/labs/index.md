# Labs Overview

Labs help learners practice quantum computing concepts through observation, simulation, reproducibility, and careful interpretation.

## Beginner Labs

- [Lab 001: Single-Qubit Measurement](./beginner/lab-001-single-qubit-measurement.md)
- [Lab 002: Shot Count Observation](./beginner/lab-002-shot-count-observation.md)

These labs support [QF-101: Quantum Thinking](/docs/quantum-foundations/qf-101-quantum-thinking). They are student-facing public labs and do not include official solutions, protected assessment checks, sample completed responses, official grading scripts, or official grading logic.
