# Labs Overview

Labs connected to enrollment-required courses are not published as full lab files in the public repository.

## Public Lab Catalogue

The public site may show lab titles and short descriptions so learners understand the course sequence. Full lab files, lab instructions, assignments, tests, submission instructions, official solutions, answer keys, model answers, grading logic, hidden tests, and reviewer notes are released only through the enrolled learner process or never published at all.
