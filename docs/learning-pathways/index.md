# Learning Pathways Overview

Goblin Quantum Lab learning pathways organize the main course sequence for practical quantum computing education.

Support Subjects are open public helper subjects. Pathway courses are enrollment-required unless a course page explicitly states otherwise.

## Current Public Catalogue

- [Course Catalogue](/docs/course-catalogue)
- [Quantum Foundations](/docs/quantum-foundations)
- [QF-101: Quantum Thinking enrollment overview](/docs/quantum-foundations/qf-101-quantum-thinking)

## Optional Support Subjects

[Support Subjects](/docs/support-subjects) are standalone helper courses. They are open to all visitors, not part of the main enrolled pathway by default, and not formal prerequisites by default.

Learners may take Support Subjects before, during, or after pathway courses. A pathway course may recommend a Support Subject as helpful preparation when a topic would make the main material easier to follow.

## Access Boundary

Public pathway pages are overviews only. Full course materials, course slides, course PDFs, lab files, assignments, tests, submission instructions, and learner-specific review instructions are released only through the enrolled learner process.
