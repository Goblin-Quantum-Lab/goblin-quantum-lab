# QF-101.5 Preview: Common Quantum Misconceptions

This public page is a short preview only. Full lesson materials are released through the enrolled learner process.

## Public Preview

This module introduces common beginner misconceptions and encourages no-hype explanations of what quantum computers, simulators, measurement, and samples can and cannot show.

## Enrolled Learners Explore

- why quantum computers are not faster at everything
- simulation versus hardware execution
- why small samples can mislead
- careful public claims about quantum experiments
