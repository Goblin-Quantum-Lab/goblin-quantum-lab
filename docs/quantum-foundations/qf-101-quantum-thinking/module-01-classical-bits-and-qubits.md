# QF-101.1 Preview: Classical Bits and Qubits

This public page is a short preview only. Full lesson materials are released through the enrolled learner process.

## Public Preview

This module introduces the difference between classical bits and qubits at a beginner level. The enrolled lesson focuses on careful language, measurement boundaries, and why a qubit should not be described as a better classical bit.

## Enrolled Learners Explore

- classical `0` and `1` states
- qubit state before measurement
- measurement outcomes
- why beginner explanations can mislead
