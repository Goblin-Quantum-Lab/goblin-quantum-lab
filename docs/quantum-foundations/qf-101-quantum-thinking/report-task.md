# QF-101 Report Task

The QF-101 report task asks you to write a short beginner observation report based on one of the QF-101 labs.

The goal is clear observation, not advanced mathematics.

## Choose One Lab

Choose one:

- [Lab 001: Single-Qubit Measurement](/docs/labs/beginner/lab-001-single-qubit-measurement)
- [Lab 002: Shot Count Observation](/docs/labs/beginner/lab-002-shot-count-observation)

## Required Sections

Your report should include:

1. **Objective**: What were you trying to observe?
2. **Expected result**: What did you expect before inspecting the final result?
3. **Method**: What did you inspect or run?
4. **Execution setting**: Where or how did the experiment run?
5. **Observed result**: What counts or result pattern did you observe?
6. **Interpretation**: What does the result support, and what does it not prove?
7. **Limitation**: What is one limitation of the experiment?
8. **Conclusion**: What did you learn?

## Length

Suggested length: 250-500 words.

Shorter is acceptable if the report is clear and complete.

## Template

Use the [Beginner Lab Report Template](/docs/report-writing/beginner-lab-report-template) if you want a simple structure to copy into your notes.
