# QF-101.2 Preview: Probability and Measurement

This public page is a short preview only. Full lesson materials are released through the enrolled learner process.

## Public Preview

This module introduces probability, measurement outcomes, repeated shots, counts, and why a single run is not enough for careful quantum experiment interpretation.

## Enrolled Learners Explore

- probability as a way to describe uncertain outcomes
- measurement as a process that produces recorded results
- shots and count dictionaries
- why small samples can look unstable
