# QF-101 Competency Check Preview

This public page is an assessment overview only. Full competency questions, tests, submission instructions, official solutions, answer keys, model answers, grading logic, and reviewer notes are never published in the public repository.

## Public Preview

The enrolled competency check asks learners to demonstrate beginner quantum experiment literacy, including careful language around qubits, measurement, probability, repeated shots, result counts, simulator discipline, limitations, and reporting.

## Access Boundary

Official assessed work is handled through the enrolled learner process. Learners must not publish assessment prompts, submissions, reviewer feedback, or protected assessment materials.

Badge and completion review is manual and may take up to 30 business days.
